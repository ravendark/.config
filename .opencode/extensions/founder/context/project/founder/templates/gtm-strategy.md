## Section Guidance

### Positioning Statement

Use Geoffrey Moore's format exactly. The constraint forces precision.

### Channel Assessment

- **CAC Est.**: Your best guess, mark as estimate
- **Scalability**: Can you 10x this channel?
- **Time to Results**: How long until meaningful data?
- **Fit Score**: How well does this match your customer?

### 90-Day Plan

- Each phase should have a clear goal
- Exit criteria are non-negotiable gates
- Weekly granularity keeps accountability

### Metrics

- North Star should be singular
- Dashboard should fit on one screen
- Include leading indicators for early signals

---

## Checklist Before Delivery

- [ ] Positioning statement is specific and differentiated
- [ ] Target customer is narrow enough to act on
- [ ] Channel strategy has clear prioritization
- [ ] 90-day plan is actionable and has exit criteria
- [ ] Metrics are measurable and have targets
- [ ] Risks are honest and have mitigations
- [ ] Next steps are concrete and assigned
